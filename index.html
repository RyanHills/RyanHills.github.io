Our Story

The First of January

Once upon a time, in the midst of a global lockdown, Ryan and Meghan found solace in each other's company. Their love story was an extraordinary one that unfolded during the most challenging of times. They shared a deep connection and a passion for exploring the world together, which was temporarily put on hold due to the pandemic.
As New Year's Eve approached, Ryan came up with a brilliant idea to propose to Meghan. With a heart full of love and anticipation, he meticulously planned the surprise proposal.
From that fateful day onward, Ryan and Meghan's love story continued to unfurl. Eagerly, they awaited the resumption of their travel adventures, yearning to explore the world hand in hand and engender countless new memories. In the interim, they clung steadfastly to the belief that love transcends the constraints of physical boundaries, vowing to cherish every passing moment until the world reopens its gates.

How They Met

Love blossomed in the digital realm for Ryan and Meghan when they crossed paths on an online dating platform in the enchanting year of 2020. Their connection was instantaneous, leading to the anticipation of a first date filled with excitement and nervous butterflies. Their inaugural rendezvous unfolded in the charming town of Eastbourne, where they shared a delightful meal, sparking a flame that would grow into something truly magical. The picturesque setting became the backdrop for the beginning of a beautiful journey together. As their story continued to unfold, the couple ventured into the world of cinema for their second date. However, the experience was not without its amusing moments. Picture this: a reel of laughter-inducing awkwardness as both Ryan and Meghan found themselves hesitating over the popcorn, untouched, and drinks left untouched. The film played in the background, providing the soundtrack to a symphony of silent glances and shared smiles. Little did they know that these seemingly awkward moments would become cherished memories, a testament to the unique and genuine connection they were building. From that point forward, their journey as a couple embraced both the magical and the amusing, laying the foundation for a love that would stand the test of time.
